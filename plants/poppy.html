Poppy

Papawer / Slaappapawer
Papaver somniferum
Toxic & Dangerous PlantsGardenpainmental

About this plant

Source of morphine and codeine — powerful pain relief in controlled medical use. Garden poppies dangerous if processed. Illegal to grow opium poppy in SA

Animal Safety Guide
