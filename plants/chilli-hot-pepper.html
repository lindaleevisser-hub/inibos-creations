Chilli / Hot Pepper

Rissie / Brandrissie
Capsicum annuum / C. frutescens
SA Herb GardenGardenpaindigestiveimmunerespiratoryskin

About this plant

Capsaicin — powerful pain reliever, metabolism booster, anti-bacterial, digestive stimulant. Used topically for arthritis, nerve pain. SA grows excellent chillies — peri-peri is a local favourite.

Animal Safety Guide

Safety information for the major species. Notes describe specific cautions where they apply.

🐎 Horse
Can irritate mouth and gut — not recommended
🐄 Cattle
Use with caution — see notes
🐑 Sheep
Use with caution — see notes
🐐 Goat
Use with caution — see notes
🐕 Dog
Causes significant digestive distress — avoid
🐈 Cat
Same as dogs
🐔 Chicken
Interestingly SAFE — birds lack the capsaicin receptor; used to deter mammal pests from feed
🐷 Pig
Use with caution — see notes
👤 Human
Fresh, dried, or powdered — excellent metabolic booster and pain relief. Peri-peri is a nutritious powerhouse.
