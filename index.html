About Linda

The woman behind Inibos Creations

Linda & friend · Rooidam Farm 🐴

Linda Visser

Rooidam Farm · Thabazimbi · South Africa

Born in Durban, raised in Newcastle in Natal — then Thabazimbi, where my children were born in the Bushveld. Then Kathu and Deben in the Northern Cape, where my rescue Kalahari Heart grew and where I truly learned the language of donkeys and the generosity of people who care.

Now, almost three decades later, I am back in the Bushveld near Thabazimbi — on Rooidam farm, owned by Aunty Ria and Oom Ben who are like grandparents to me — doing exactly what I love, with my animals and my art. Happy and blessed. 🌿

That journey gave me knowledge no book could. I have lost animals to plants I didn't recognise. I have saved animals because I found information in time. I have sat with grieving donkeys and understood their pain as real. Inibos Creations is my way of giving that knowledge back.
