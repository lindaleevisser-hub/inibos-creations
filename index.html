About Linda

Where the stories come from

I live on Rooidam farm in South Africa, surrounded by donkeys, dusty mornings, and the kind of beauty that only makes sense once you've also survived the heartbreak. My companions — Dixie, Luna, and Klaradyn — have taught me more than any book ever could.

For seven years I ran Kalahari Heart Rescue in the Northern Cape and Limpopo, giving everything to creatures who could only ask for help with their eyes. These books carry those stories — the mud and the miracles, the grief and the grace.

Whether you keep donkeys, love them from afar, or simply need a quiet space to write and reflect — there is something here for you.
