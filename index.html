About Linda

The woman behind Inibos Creations

Linda & friend · Rooidam Farm 🐴

Linda Visser

Rooidam Farm · Northern Cape · South Africa

I grew up in the Kalahari and Bushveld — the land where my son and daughter were born. For years I ran a rescue in Deben, Northern Cape, where I learned the language of donkeys and the generosity of people who care. Then life took me elsewhere. Twenty-four years later, I am back — on Rooidam farm, owned by Aunty Ria and Oom Ben who are like grandparents to me — doing exactly what I love, with my animals and my art.

That journey from Kalahari to Bushveld, from rescue to farm, from young woman to grandmother — it gave me knowledge I couldn't have gotten from any book. I have lost animals to plants I didn't recognise. I have saved animals because I found information in time. I have sat with grieving donkeys and understood their pain as real.

Inibos Creations is my way of giving that knowledge back. The databases are free. The books are coming. And every single thing here was built with love from Rooidam farm. 🌿
